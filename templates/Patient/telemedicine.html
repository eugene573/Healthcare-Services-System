What is Telemedicine?

Telemedicine allows you to consult with your healthcare provider remotely from the comfort of your home using digital technology. This means you can have a doctor's appointment using your smartphone, tablet, or computer without needing to visit the clinic in person.

How Telemedicine Works?

1.Scheduling an Appointment: Choose a time that fits your schedule.

2.Preparing for the Visit: Make sure you have a good internet connection and a device with a camera and microphone. Find a quiet and private place for your appointment.

3. During the Appointment: Connect with your doctor via video call. Discuss your symptoms, ask questions, and get medical advice.

4. Follow-Up: Receive prescriptions electronically, get referrals to specialists, and schedule follow-up appointments if needed.

Common Uses of Telemedicine

1.General Consultations: For common illnesses like colds, flu, and minor infections.

2.Chronic Disease Management: Ongoing care for conditions like diabetes.

3. Post-Surgery Follow-Ups: Quick and convenient follow-up visits to monitor recovery after surgery.
